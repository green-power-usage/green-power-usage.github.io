Climate change is one of the most pressing issues facing the world today. Wasteful practices and pollution have led to a warming atmosphere and an increasingly hostile environment. While this is caused by the advanced technology we use today, this technology can help us prevent climate change as well. Through the developement of green energy we can reduce waste and pollution and preserve the environment we live in. Many organizations, particularly in the United States, claim to be doing a lot to persue green energy and less carbon emissions, but how much are they really doing? We have looked at multiple studies and their findings for green energy usage among college campuses in the United States, and done our own data analysis and observations. Our results and analysis are compiled into the data and figures presented on this site.

The above map is a representation of the data set that we used for our research. Each point represents one of the data points, being a college in a particular year, and gives the data on the green energy usage as well as the green power resources.
